小妇人/LITTLE WOMEN

简介/INTRODUCE BRIEFLY

《小妇人》是美国作家 Louisa May Alcott 写的长篇小说，以19世纪中期美国内战时期为背景，讲述了马奇一家在丈夫参战后马奇夫人陪伴四个女儿度过童年和青春期的故事。

Little Women is a novel written by American writer Louisa May Alcott. It is set in the American Civil War period in the mid-19th century and tells the story of the March family and how Mrs. March accompanied her four daughters through their childhood and adolescence after her husband went to war.
