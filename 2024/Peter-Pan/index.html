Hello, everyone. I'm Ji Boyu. I come from Class 10, Grade 7 of Hongqiao Experimental Middle School.
大家好，我是 Ji Boyu。我来自 Hongqiao Experimental Middle School 的七 (10) 班。
Today I'm going to tell you about an interesting book, named Peter Pan.
今天，我要向你介绍一本有趣的书，叫作《彼得·潘》
Now, let's get down to business!
现在，让我们进入正题吧！
"Peter Pan"
《彼得·潘》
Written by James Matthew Barrie in Scotland.
这本书是苏格兰作家詹姆斯·马修·巴利
A fairy tale about a boy Peter Pan, who doesn't want to grow up, leads Wendy and her brothers to go on an adventure on Neverland.
一个童话故事，讲述了一个不想长大的男孩彼得潘带领温迪和她的兄弟们在梦幻岛冒险
Although these seventy-seven pages of stories are easy to read. But every time I read it, the meaning changes.
虽然这本书只有短短77页，内容也很简单，但我每次重新读这本书，带给我的感受都不一样。
Now I also want to share some of my personal thoughts about this book.
现在我也想和大家分享一些我个人的一些想法
They duel with pirates, befriend Native Americans, and explore unknown territories, all the while reveling in the joy of being carefree.
他们与海盗搏斗，与印地安人交朋友，探索者未知的领域，享受着无忧无虑的快乐
However, when Wendy grows up and chooses the life of an adult, Peter Pan falls into deep sorrow.
然而当温迪长大并选择成年人的生活时，彼得潘却陷入了深深的悲伤。
He can't accept this reality, for he harbors a wish in his heart to never grow up.
他无法接受这个事实，因为他心中始终有一个不愿长大的愿望。
Reading this, I can't help but reflect deeply. Peter Pan's story reminds me of everyone's childhood. Back then, we were innocent and filled with curiosity and desire for the unknown world. We dreamed of being heroes, embarking on adventures, and saving the world.
读到这里，我不禁陷入深思。彼得·潘的故事让我想起了我们每个人的童年。那时，我们天真无邪，充满了对未知世界的好奇和渴望。我们梦想着成为英雄，去探险、去拯救世界。
Yet, as we age, we become bound by reality, taking on various responsibilities and obligations.
然而，随着年龄的增长，我们逐渐被现实所束缚，开始承担起各种责任和义务。
We gradually forget that dream-filled self, becoming more mature and realistic.
我们逐渐忘记了那个曾经怀揣梦想的自己，变得越来越成熟，越来越现实。
Nevertheless, Peter Pan's tale tells us that even as time passes, we should not forget the dreams and pursuits in our hearts. We should maintain a youthful spirit like Peter Pan, bravely chasing our dreams. For it is only in this way that we can find true happiness and fulfillment on life's journey.
不过，彼得·潘的故事告诉我们，即使岁月流逝，我们也不应该忘记内心的梦想和追求。我们应该项彼得·潘一样，永远保持一颗年轻的心，去勇敢地追求自己的梦想。因为只有这样，我们才能在生活的旅途中找到真正的快乐和满足
Of course, we must also understand that growing up is an irreversible process. We cannot stay in childhood forever, nor can we always flee from reality.
当然，我们也要明白，成长是一个不可逆转的过程，我们不能一直停留在童年，也不能一直逃避现实。
We need to learn to face life's challenges and difficulties, to grow and progress.
我们需要学会面对生活的挑战和困难，去成长、去进步。
It's a fun read and good book to improve your vocabulary.
这是一本很有趣的好书可以用来提升你的英文词汇量。
So, if you are a beginner who wants to read English books, I definitely recommend you to start with this book.
所以，如果你是新手，刚刚准备开始读英文书，我强烈推荐你可以从这本书开始读。
